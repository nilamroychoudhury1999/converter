Temperature Converter

This temperature converter can convert between different temperature units, you just need to enter a original value and select the original unit in combobox, converter will quickly calculate all the values of other temperature units. How To Use: 1.Input a valid numeric. 2.Select an original Temperature unit, such as Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin . The calculation results will be displayed in table Temperature unit Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin .
